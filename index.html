Fighting Styles & Martial Arts

Fighting includes various combat styles developed for self-defense, competition, and discipline.

Boxing

A combat sport focused on punches, footwork, and head movement.

Muay Thai

Known as the "Art of Eight Limbs", it uses fists, elbows, knees, and shins.

Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu

A grappling-based martial art focusing on ground control and submissions.

Taekwondo

Korean martial art emphasizing high kicks, fast movement, and discipline.
